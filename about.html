About Me

Hello!

I’m Shelby Dixon — a former organic farmer turned Computer Engineering student at UH Manoa. I specialize in AI, robotics, cybersecurity, and hardware acceleration. Currently, I’m building AI-enhanced Raspberry Pi robots and leading the Mobius Camera payload system for NASA's RockSat-X mission.

Before engineering, I worked on organic farms in Hawaiʻi and Northern California, experiences that taught me problem-solving, patience, and sustainability. Today, I bring those values into my academic research, design challenges, and collaborations.

Outside of tech, I enjoy traveling to new places, baking, and spending time with my dog. I also work as a physics lab assistant on campus and love helping others learn hands-on science and engineering skills.
